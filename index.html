Original Rules

There is one Leader.
The Leader does not know the location, but they know whether the location is on land, air, or water.
The non-spies know who the Leader is. They are trying to tell the Leader where the place is without telling the Spy.

Spy wins if they can guess the location or who the Leader is.

Non-spies win if they can guess the spy
OR
if the Leader can guess the place.
